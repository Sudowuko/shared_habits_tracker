# Shared Habits Tracker

### Welcome!

The purpose of this project is to allow users to create their own daily habits and participate compete in friendly team-based competition to see who comes out on top!

To start, use /changehabit to make your new habit!

<img width="519" alt="Screenshot 2023-06-19 at 7 05 27 PM" src="https://github.com/Sudowuko/shared_habits_tracker/assets/52169399/3acc8133-8aef-46db-b0a6-3e5beaeb4f7b">

All participating users will get sorted into equal teams, and your current team can be seen using /stats

<img width="315" alt="Screenshot 2023-06-19 at 7 09 11 PM" src="https://github.com/Sudowuko/shared_habits_tracker/assets/52169399/690da538-a369-4c49-a86f-dee8d96feb32">

To log your own habit for the day, simply click on your team button to mark it as completed!
